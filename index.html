About Ahavin Consultancy

Ahavin was established in 2026 as a professional Civil, Architecture & Construction consultancy Engineering firm under the leadership of. Shovan Kundu and Team of Ahavin.

Ahavin Consultancy Services provides engineering consultancy solutions including Architectural Planning, Structural Design, Bridge & Highway Engineering and Project estimation and Management.

Our mission is to deliver safe, innovative, residential, cost efficient design solutions for residential, commercial and infrastructure projects (Factory, PEB) in Bengal and beyond.
